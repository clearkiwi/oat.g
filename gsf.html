Illinois Sinkhole Swallows Soccer Field

Well the people of the southern Illinois town of Alton will not be playing soccer anytime soon!
On Wednesday, June 26th Alton's Gordon Moore Park soccer field fell into a giant sinkhole!
Most likely due to it being built on a limestone mine. Luckily no one was hurt.
The park has been closed indefinitely. Photo by John Badman.
